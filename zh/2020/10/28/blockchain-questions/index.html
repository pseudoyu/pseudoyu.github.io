Blockchain Technology and Applications

BitCoin System (Transaction-based ledger)

Additional questions

  • Q: In a transaction, the receiver is not connected to the bitcoin network.
    • A: No need online, just records
  • Q: Lost private key
    • A: No solution
    • Exchanges: Mt. Gox
  • Q: Wrong address
    • A: No solution
  • Q: OP_RETURN, return mistake, why it’s still executed?
    • A: OP_RETURN is in the output, so it will be verified in the next TX
  • Q: How to judge who find the nonce first?
    • A: Coinbase tx in the block
  • Q: How to judge who will get the tx fee?
    • A: No need to know in advance, just calculate (output - input)
  • Q: How Hash pointer work in other hosts (pointer only works locally)?
    • A: Only Hash, no pointer (stored in key-value database, e.g. levelDB)
  • Q: Divide private key to several pieces?
    • A: Security, loss
    • Possible solution: MULTISIG
  • Q: How can Bitcoin gets its consensus?
    • A: Actually no consensus, like fork attack, in principle/in practice
  • Q: How to attrack miners?
    • A: Increase profits and lower cost, rareness
  • Q: Will quantum computing influence Bitcoin?
    • A: Not in practical use
